About Us

A multi-service enterprise built on trust and performance.

Vivri Ventures is a dynamic multi-service and supply enterprise founded with the vision of delivering reliable, high-quality solutions across India. Headquartered in Jammu, Jammu & Kashmir, we specialize in versatile operations that adapt to the evolving needs of clients across sectors.

Whether it's government contracts, private partnerships, logistics support, or procurement facilitation — our team brings dedication, compliance, and a proven vendor network to every engagement.

Deliver

01

Logistics & Transportation Support

Office & Industrial Supplies
